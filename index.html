About

Fifty years of business experience across four continents. Graduated at 21 with an English Literature degree, became CEO of a design and manufacturing company by 23, spent decades in boardrooms from Texas to Hong Kong to El Salvador, grew companies, managed global operations. Lifelong motorcyclist, licensed bartender, perpetual traveler. Now based in Santa Fe.

Stepped away from the corporate world at 70. Now I write, podcast with my son Griffin about Gen Z masculinity, curate ideas, and help companies with strategic assessments and navigating AI options. Back to where I started—just with better stories.
