Rooted in experience, voiced through story

Barry is a Santa Fe-based writer and artist who previously led multinational retail and manufacturing companies across four continents. He now focuses on translating decades of executive insight into accessible storytelling, father–son dialogue, and visual art that navigates chaos and renewal.

Words on the page

Essays and reflections on leadership, identity, and how families build resilient creative practice. Barry’s latest writing blends the personal tone of a journal with the pragmatism of a seasoned strategist.

Voices across generations

The Malestrum podcast shares real conversations between Barry and his son Griffin about business, pop culture, and finding meaning as two generations navigate modern life.

Art in motion

From abstract canvases to sketches on the road, Barry documents movement, thresholds, and luminous color. Artwork here provides a visual counterweight to the written word.
